### Aesop

[top](#table-of-contents)

For: Animal Handling  
Base: Spiritus  
Ingredients: 1x Belladonna, 1x Boar's Tusk, 2x Comfrey

stat_chg: +5 Nourishment, +3 Energy, 0 Health  
stat_pct: 90% Nutrition, 6% Alcohol  
stat_wgt: 0.2

Where recipe: Pschitoky Apothecary  
Where herbs:

#### Aesop effects

- Weak: for 6 hours
  - Horsemanship, Houndmaster +3
  - "Animals will take less notice of you, so you'll find it easier to approach them"

- Normal: for 12 hours
  - Horsemanship, Houndmaster +3
  - "Animals will take less notice of you, so you'll find it easier to approach them"

- Strong: for 12 hours
  - Horsemanship, Houndmaster +5
  - "Animals will take less notice of you, so you'll find it easier to approach them"

- Henry: for 24 hours
  - Horsemanship, Houndmaster +7
  - "Animals will take less notice of you, so you'll find it easier to approach them"

#### Aesop OoOps

Prep: Spiritus, 1x Belladonna, 1x Boar's Tusk, 2x Comfrey

1) Spirits & lower cauldron
2) 2x Comfrey -> mortar
3) Grind Comfrey
4) Comfrey -> bowl
5) Comfrey -> cauldron
6) 1x Boar Tusk -> cauldron
7) 1x Belladonna -> mortar
8) Grind Belladonna
9) Belladonna -> cauldron
10) Lift cauldron
11) 1x Vial
12) Distill
